Built for The King's Fans

Everything a LeBron fan needs, wrapped in a clean, dark-themed music player experience.

🎵

49+ Parody Songs

A massive collection of LeBron-themed parodies of popular songs - from "Dunk With a Smile" to "He Is The King" and everything in between.

👑

LeBron's Pick of the Day

Every day, The King himself picks a song just for you. A new featured track rotates daily to keep things fresh.

🔀

Let The King Decide

Can't choose? Hit the shuffle button and let LeBron pick your next banger. The King always knows best.

🏀

Playbooks

Create custom playlists (Playbooks) with your favorite tracks. Add custom cover art and organize your lineup like a championship roster.

⭐

All-Star Favorites

Crown your favorite songs as All-Stars. Build your dream team of parody hits and access them instantly from The Vault.

AD Breaks

Anthony Davis pops up with hilarious "ad" breaks between songs. Trade him to Dallas or put him on injured reserve to dismiss!

Taco Tuesday Mode

🎧

Full Music Player

Shuffle, repeat, queue management, progress scrubbing - a complete music experience with "On The Bench" queue preview.

🚗

CarPlay & Widgets

Take LeBron on the road with CarPlay support. Live Activities and home screen widgets keep the music at your fingertips.

MVP Badges

Starting 5

Now Playing

A gorgeous full-screen player with dynamic gradient backgrounds that shift with each song. Designed for the ultimate listening experience:

  • Large Album Art - Beautiful cover art with shadow effects
  • Scrub Bar - Drag to seek with a thumb that appears on touch

  • Action Row - All-Star (favorite), Queue, Playbook (add to playlist), Mix (random queue)
  • On The Bench - See what's coming up next in the queue
  • Empty State - A special LeBron quote when nothing is playing

The Vault

Your personal music library, organized with basketball flair. Everything is stored securely in The Vault:

  • Custom Covers - Upload your own playlist cover images
  • Sort Options - By title, artist, play count, or most recent
  • Draft a Playbook - Create new playlists with the yellow action button

Anthony Davis "AD" Breaks

Between songs, Anthony Davis pops up with random hilarious interruptions. Each one has a unique title, pose, and message. Dismiss them with basketball-themed buttons like:

  • "Trade AD to Dallas"
  • "Send AD to the Bench"
  • "AD Fouled Out - Skip"
  • "Put AD on Injured Reserve"

Taco Tuesday Mode

Every Tuesday, the entire app transforms! Inspired by LeBron's iconic Taco Tuesday celebrations:

  • Falling taco rain animation across the screen
  • Special "TACO TUESDAYYYYY" banner with gradient
  • Auto-plays the Taco Tuesday song on launch
  • Taco rain returns every time the song plays
  • Special splash screen message

The Chalk Toss

MVP Badges

Your most-played songs earn their spot on the podium. Golden MVP badges appear directly on album art throughout the app:

  • Gold #1 badge - Your most-played song of all time
  • Gold #2 badge - Your runner-up track
  • Gold #3 badge - Bronze position on the podium
  • Badges update in real-time as play counts change
  • Visible on Home, Queue, and Playlist screens
